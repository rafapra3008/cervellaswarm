## Appendix: The Shortwave Method (Workflow Framework)

**Core Principle:** "Only handle it once" (OHIO)

### Step-by-Step

1. **Complete triage pass** before other work
   - Don't start tasks until ALL email processed

2. **Classify each email** (3 categories only):
   - **Non-actionable:** Done/Delete/Snooze immediately
   - **Quick (<2min):** Handle NOW (use AI instant replies)
   - **Tasks (>2min):** Star (quick) or Todo (complex)

3. **Process top-to-bottom** sequentially
   - Don't jump around
   - Each email = immediate decision

4. **Remove from inbox** after handling
   - Archive, done, or snooze
   - Goal: Inbox = only actionable tasks

5. **Organize remaining** by priority
   - Drag-and-drop todos
   - Stars for quick-reference

### Tools They Provide for Method

- **Bundles:** Handle 10 newsletters in 1 click
- **Splits:** Separate importance levels visually
- **AI instant replies:** 2min ‚Üí 10sec
- **Delivery schedules:** Control when email arrives
- **Tasklet (automation):** Handle routine before you see them

**Philosophy:** Inbox = chef's workspace. Clean before you cook.
