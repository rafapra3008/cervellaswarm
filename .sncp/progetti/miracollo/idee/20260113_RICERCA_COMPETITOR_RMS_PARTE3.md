> "Black box systems lead to mistrust, overrides, algorithmic aversion"

**Stakeholder Requirements:**

## Trust & AI Adoption

### Trust Building Pattern

**Stage 1: Skepticism (Week 1-4)**
- Review every suggestion
- Override frequently
- Test AI understanding

**Stage 2: Validation (Month 2-3)**
- Compare AI vs manual
- Selectively trust
- Autopilot for simple scenarios

**Stage 3: Trust (Month 4+)**
- Full autopilot
- Strategic oversight only
- 99% acceptance rate

**Atomize Data:**
> "Over time, nearly ALL users transition to autopilot"
> Initial hesitation → eventual trust.
> Performance = trust builder.
