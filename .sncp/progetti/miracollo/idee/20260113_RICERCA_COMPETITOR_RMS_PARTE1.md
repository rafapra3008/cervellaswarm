**Tutti hanno autopilot:**

## Market Positioning - Dove Sono

### Enterprise Tier (€500-5000+/mese)

**IDeaS G3 RMS**
- Target: Luxury hotels, catene, enterprise
- Rating: 92% da 355 Luxury Hotels
- Strengths: 30,000+ proprietà, SAS analytics, 225+ integrazioni
- Weaknesses: Costoso, complesso, troppo per SMB

**Duetto**
- Target: Mid-market, boutique hotels
- Rating: #1 HotelTechAwards 2022-2025
- Strengths: Cloud-native, UX eccellente, HotStats acquisition
- Weaknesses: Pricing alto, non SMB-first

### Mid-Market Tier (€300-500/mese)

**Atomize**
- Target: SMB, independent hotels (20+ rooms)
- Rating: #3 HotelTechAwards 2025
- Pricing: €299+/mese
- Strengths: AI 24/7, autopilot trusted, +15-20% RevPAR
- Weaknesses: Basic UI, no transparent AI

**TakeUp**
- Target: Independent hotels, boutique, B&B
- Funding: $11M Series A (Luglio 2025)
- Strengths: "Why This Rate", transparent AI, +20% revenue
- Weaknesses: Nuovo player, meno clienti

### SMB Tier (€100-300/mese)

**RoomPriceGenie**
- Target: Small/mid independent hotels, B&B
- Rating: #2 HotelTechAwards 2025 (SMB winner!)
- Pricing: €119-449/mese (4 tiers)
- Strengths: Easy setup, +19% revenue, 10h/week saving
- Weaknesses: 7 update/giorno (vs 24/7), less advanced AI

### Vacation Rental (Non competitor diretto)

**Beyond Pricing**
- Target: Airbnb, Vrbo, vacation rentals
- Strengths: Neyoba AI, +40% profits claim
- Note: Non hotel focus, ma interessante per AI approach
